HTML & WEB DESIGN DASHBOARD

Welcome to this course!!!

Select a module from the sidebar to begin.

Course Description: This dynamic course introduces Grade 10 learners to the world of web development through the foundational coding language: HTML (HyperText Markup Language).

Designed to align with the South African curriculum and 21st-century digital skills, the course empowers learners to understand, design, and build their own webpages and basic websites from scratch.

Learners will explore the structure and components of HTML, including elements, tags, attributes, hyperlinks, images, tables, and forms. Through hands-on activities and real-world projects, they will develop practical skills in planning website layouts, writing clean and functional code, and viewing their work in web browsers.

This course encourages creativity, logical thinking, and problem-solving while promoting digital literacy. By the end of the course, learners will have built their own functional multi-page website, laying a strong foundation for further studies in coding, Information Technology, or digital entrepreneurship.

Outcomes of this course:

    Understand the purpose and structure of HTML.
      Create and format web content using HTML tags and elements.
        Incorporate images, links, tables, and multimedia into webpages.
          Design a basic multi-page website using effective layout principles.
            Develop confidence in coding and digital communication.

            Target Audience:

            Grade 10 learners with an interest in technology, digital design, or coding.
